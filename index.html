Luxury Hotel

Luxury Hotel offers the elite standard for guests desiring the ultimate in luxurious, retreat accommodation. Offering thoughtfully designed rooms and immaculately maintained facilities, the hotel provides total peace and tranquility in a delightfully secluded setting.

Accommodation

Situated only thirty minutes from Nairobi city, Luxury hotel is easily accessible for both international and domestic guests. The Hotel's location provides both a gateway to those wishing to sample Kenya world-class dining and entertainment culture, and for our guests seeking a peaceful retreat.
